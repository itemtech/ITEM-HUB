Admission at Kijomoro Secondary School

Start your journey with a school committed to academic excellence, character development, and community leadership. Read the admission requirements, application steps, and support options for new learners.

Step-by-step admission support

Admission requirements

Applicants should be prepared to join our learning community with the right documentation and a strong willingness to grow. Here is the information needed for a successful application.

  • Completed application form with parent or guardian details
  • Certified copy of previous school report or transfer letter
  • Birth certificate or national ID copy for age verification
  • Two passport photographs and contact information for guardians

School fees

Kijomoro Secondary School offers a clear and affordable fee structure for day and boarding students. Fees are shown separately for O-Level and A-Level students.

  • O-Level day students (Forms 1–4): UGX 199,660 – 200,000
  • O-Level boarding students (Forms 1–4): UGX 326,600
  • A-Level day students (Forms 5–6): UGX 180,600
  • A-Level boarding students (Forms 5–6): UGX 326,600

Application steps

  1. Download the application form or collect it from our school office.
  2. Complete the form with accurate personal and academic details.
  3. Attach the required documents and submit them to the admissions desk.
  4. Attend a brief interview or orientation session with school staff.
  5. Receive your admission decision and prepare for the new academic term.

Why choose Kijomoro S.S?

We blend strong academics with extracurriculars, leadership training, and a supportive environment. Our students grow as learners, athletes, and responsible citizens.
