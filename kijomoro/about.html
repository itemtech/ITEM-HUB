About Kijomoro Secondary School

Since our foundation, Kijomoro S.S has been dedicated to fostering academic excellence, character development, and community leadership. Our commitment is to empower every learner with the knowledge, skills, and values needed for success.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide high-quality education that develops critical thinkers, compassionate leaders, and responsible citizens committed to building a better society for all.

✨

Our Vision

A school where every student succeeds academically, grows as a person, and prepares to make a positive impact in their communities and the wider world.

Core Values

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in academic achievement and personal growth.

Integrity

We uphold honesty, responsibility, and ethical conduct in all our actions.

Community

We foster belonging, collaboration, and mutual respect among all members.

Innovation

We embrace modern teaching methods and encourage creative problem-solving.

Resilience

We build perseverance and the courage to overcome challenges and grow.

Inclusivity

We celebrate diversity and provide equal opportunities for all learners.

Academic Structure

O-Level (Forms 1–4)

Foundation years focused on core academics including mathematics, languages, sciences, and social studies. Students develop essential skills and knowledge to prepare for advanced studies.

A-Level (Forms 5–6)

Specialized advanced coursework in science, commerce, and humanities streams. Students pursue in-depth study of chosen subjects to prepare for university and professional careers.

Academic Programs

Science & Technology

Cutting-edge labs and hands-on learning in physics, chemistry, biology, and information technology to inspire tomorrow's innovators.

Languages & Literature

Rigorous instruction in English, Kiswahili, and foreign languages combined with creative writing and critical analysis.

Mathematics & Engineering

Comprehensive curriculum developing quantitative reasoning and applied problem-solving for real-world applications.

Sports & Arts

Extracurricular excellence in athletics, music, drama, and visual arts to nurture well-rounded learners.

Spiritual & Religious Life

Kijomoro S.S is a secular institution that respects and celebrates the diverse religious and spiritual beliefs of our entire student community. We provide inclusive religious activities and observances for all faiths, including Christianity, Islam, Judaism, Hinduism, Buddhism, and other religious traditions. Our chaplaincy program offers pastoral care, faith-based clubs, prayer spaces, and interfaith dialogue opportunities that help students explore their spirituality while fostering mutual respect and understanding. Whether through worship, meditation, scripture study, or spiritual reflection, we support the religious growth and well-being of every learner. We believe that spiritual development and ethical values are central to character building and help our students become compassionate, responsible citizens.
